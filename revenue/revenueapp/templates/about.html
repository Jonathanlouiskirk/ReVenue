About

We are Chris, Jon K., and Jon T., and we love music!

We wanted to create an app that allows users to rate venues in the Austin area based on different categories. We were inspired by music blogs and other websites, as well as by our own interests in music and how the world intereacts with it. Music venues are once again a cultural hub that bring people together, and need our support if they are to thrive and succeed. We are excited to develop this app that will allow users to share their experiences with others.

ReVENUE allows the user to rate the seating, sound, scene, bathroom, and overall experience of a particular venue. There are several Austin city-area venues to select for a rating. reVENUE is setup to allow for multiple users to interact with it, by signing in with their own unique user name. reVENUE is scalabled to be used for other cities and multiple venues!

Let us know what you think of our app. We welcome your feedback!
